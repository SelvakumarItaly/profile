About Me

Welcome to My Professional Profile

I am Selvakumar Thakshnamurthy, a PhD research student at the University of Catania, specializing in enhancing document management and data integrity through distributed ledger technology and blockchain applications. With a robust background in data security, particularly in healthcare emergency services, I have a proven track record of leveraging cutting-edge technology to improve data transparency, security, and authenticity.

My journey in the field of science began with my Bachelor's in Science from Madras Christian College, followed by a Master's in Computer Application from the University of Madras. Over the years, I have honed my skills in blockchain, data analysis, and cloud computing, which I have applied in various professional roles at Clarivate Analytics and Thomson Reuters.

My current research focuses on designing blockchain-enabled systems for healthcare emergency services, which has the potential to revolutionize the way emergency data is managed and secured. I am passionate about integrating AI into my applications to further enhance their capabilities and ensure that critical healthcare data remains secure and reliable.
