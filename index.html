About Me

Welcome to My Professional Profile

I am Dr. Selvakumar Thakshnamurthy, a PhD graduate from the University of Catania, specializing in document management and data integrity using distributed ledger technology and blockchain applications. With expertise in data security, particularly in healthcare emergency services, I have a proven track record of leveraging advanced technologies to enhance data transparency, security, and authenticity.

My academic journey began with a Bachelor's in Science from Madras Christian College, followed by a Master's in Computer Application from the University of Madras. Over the years, I have gained extensive experience in blockchain, data analysis, and cloud computing, applying these skills in professional roles at Clarivate Analytics and Thomson Reuters.

My research has focused on designing blockchain-enabled systems for healthcare emergency services, aiming to revolutionize emergency data management and security. I am passionate about integrating AI into my applications to enhance their efficiency and ensure the security and reliability of critical healthcare data.
