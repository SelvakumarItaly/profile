Research Experience

PhD Research at University of Catania

Project Title: Enhancing Document Management and Data Integrity through Distributed Ledger Technology and Blockchain Applications

My research aims to design and implement a distributed software infrastructure for secure document management, focusing on transparency, security, and authenticity using blockchain technology. Key responsibilities and achievements include:

  • System Design and Development: Designed and developed a blockchain-enabled system for healthcare emergency services.
  • Requirements Gathering: Conducted comprehensive sessions with stakeholders to meet specific needs of emergency healthcare services.
  • Prototype Implementation and Testing: Developed and tested a functional prototype, evaluating performance, scalability, and usability.
  • Real-World Application: Applied the system in a real-world healthcare emergency setting, demonstrating tangible benefits.
  • Data Examination and Validation: Analyzed data to validate system effectiveness in improving emergency response coordination.

PhD Exchange Research at Newcastle University

During my exchange program at Newcastle University, I focused on leveraging blockchain technology to enhance ambulance dispatch efficiency and ensure data integrity in healthcare emergency services. Key responsibilities and achievements include:

  • Implemented a simulated research design to improve ambulance dispatch efficiency.
  • Developed smart contracts on the Ethereum Blockchain for secure patient data storage.
  • Enhanced teaching and pedagogical skills through the "Introduction to Learning and Teaching" workshop.
